Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rental services

What is your rental period?

Our standard rental period is 24 hours for most items. For weekend events, Friday delivery typically includes Saturday pickup at no additional charge. Extended rental periods are available for an additional fee.

Do you offer delivery and setup?

Yes! We provide delivery, setup, and pickup services for all rentals. Our team will ensure everything is placed according to your floor plan and properly set up. Delivery fees vary based on location and order size.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as soon as your event date is confirmed, especially for weddings and large events during peak seasons (spring and fall). A minimum of 2 weeks' notice is required for most rentals, but last-minute availability may vary.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 14 days before the event will receive a full refund. Cancellations between 7-14 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 7 days of the event are non-refundable but may be eligible for rescheduling.

How are your items cleaned?

All items are professionally cleaned and inspected after each use. Linens are commercially laundered, hard surfaces are sanitized, and all items undergo quality checks before being rented again. We adhere to strict hygiene protocols.
